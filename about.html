Our Story

In 2004, with a vision to revolutionize the IT landscape in India, Mr. Jeevan Pandey founded Red Square Infosys in the heart of Delhi/Noida's technology corridor.

What started as a passionate endeavor to bridge the gap between traditional business operations and modern technology solutions has evolved into a premier IT consultancy serving enterprises across India.

Our journey has been marked by continuous innovation, from pioneering Tally ERP-9 implementations to developing cutting-edge SaaS solutions that empower businesses to achieve unprecedented growth.

Today, we stand as a testament to the power of vision, dedication, and technological excellence, having transformed over 200+ enterprises and delivered 500+ successful projects.
